## Risk Assessment: **CRITICAL**

### Critical Issues Requiring Immediate Attention

1. **Double booking vulnerability — check-then-act race condition** (backend\src\services\bookingComConnector.js)
   At line 40 in backend\src\services\bookingComConnector.js: Availability is checked and then a booking is created in separate operations without locking. Two concurrent requests for the same room/time can both pass the availability check and both create bookings (TOCTOU race condition).
