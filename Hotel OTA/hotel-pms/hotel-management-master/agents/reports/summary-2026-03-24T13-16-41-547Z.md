## Risk Assessment: **CRITICAL**

### Critical Issues Requiring Immediate Attention

1. **Double booking vulnerability — check-then-act race condition** (backend\src\routes\testCheckouts.js)
   At line 382 in backend\src\routes\testCheckouts.js: Availability is checked and then a booking is created in separate operations without locking. Two concurrent requests for the same room/time can both pass the availability check and both create bookings (TOCTOU race condition).

1. **Bulk updateMany without tenant scope** (backend\src\routes\webhooks.js)
   At line 111 in backend\src\routes\webhooks.js: Bulk operation "updateMany" does not scope by hotelId. This affects ALL tenants' data — a deleteMany without hotelId could wipe records across all hotels.
